Kinesphere

An augmented interactive scene for dance performance, October 2019

About

A melancholic dancer wanders around a scene surrounded by iluminated hanging veils. Interactive visuals of life and the natural elements highlights their pensive performance.

Tech details

Projections made in TouchDesigner with a kinect sensor, LEDs controlled by an arduino.
