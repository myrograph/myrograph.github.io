Echec et taf

A short film about a man trapped in an enigmatic labyrinth… (in French), January 2017

About

Damien is a young man looking for work. He applies for a vague job offer which leads him into a series of puzzles that he did not expect at all.

Tech details

Edited in premiere pro and after effects.

Credits

Echec et taf was made at Digital Campus Montpellier School by 5 students in 1 months – Manuel Calueba, Alexandre Troin, Fabien Gibert, Anouk Plagnol and myself, Olivier Meyer. It won best film award at DC ESG ESARC film festival.

What was my part in this project?

  • Scenario, storyboard
  • Realisation, boom operator
  • Sound editing
  • Color correction, Final Cut
