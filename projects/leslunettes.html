Les lunettes

A short film about a kid who develops special powers… (in French), December 2018

About

On the way back from school, Léo finds a pair of shiny glasses which seem to grant him special powers… Will they help him in his relationship with his mother?

Tech details

Shot on Canon 6D, edited in premiere pro and after effects.

Credits

Les lunettes was made as a cinema project at IMAC school by 5 students in 2 months – Myriam Anik, Sami Belaidi, Nicolas Senecal, Cécile Rousset and myself, Olivier Meyer.
