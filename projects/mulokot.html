Mulokot

A contemplative game to explore along with a tale (in French), December 2018

About

This environment was built as plausible as possible, featuring endemic species of French Guiana such as the turtle liana, the cathedral tree or the awara palm tree. The video tells the traditional Amerindian tale of the Mulokot water spirit, which lures curious children into the dark water of a creek.

The game is available on itch.io.

Tech details

We tried to get the most of the Unity engine in order to have a dense forest with a sufficient amount of detail. We used the terrain tool with its procedural tree generator, various particle systems and render settings.
