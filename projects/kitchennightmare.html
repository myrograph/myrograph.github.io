Kitchen Nightmare

A VR game about doing the dishes, October 2017

About

Pile up the dishes in your tray and throw them as fast as you can in the dishwasher. The higher the pile, the bigger the combo!

You can find the executable of the game on Itch (tested on Vive)

Tech details

This game was made in Unity with VRTK - Virtual Reality Toolkit.

Credits

Kitchen Nightmare was made in 48 hours during the Virtual Game Jam 2017 by a team of 5.
